Daily Workflow

Recommended order for smooth day-to-day work and accurate day-end reporting.

  1. Record incoming stock in the Purchase Sheet.
  2. Capture goods returned in the Returns Sheet.
  3. Review opening, goods delivered, goods damaged, outstanding/BAL and closing in the Balance Sheet.
  4. Confirm available stock in the Summary Sheet.
