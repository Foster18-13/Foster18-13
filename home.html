Daily Workflow

Recommended order for accurate day-end reporting.

  1. Record incoming stock in the Purchase Sheet.
  2. Capture dispatch/load quantities in the Recording Sheet.
  3. Review opening, goods loaded, goods damaged, outstanding/BAL and closing in the Balance Sheet.
  4. Confirm available stock in the Summary Sheet.
